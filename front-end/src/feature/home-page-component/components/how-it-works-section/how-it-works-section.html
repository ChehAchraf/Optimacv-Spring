Process

Resume optimization in 3 simple steps

From upload to interview—streamlined, transparent, and powered by AI.

Upload & Target

Upload your resume as a PDF and paste the job description. OptimaCV links the two so every suggestion is tailored to that specific role.

2

AI Deep Analysis

The AI scans for keywords, skill gaps, and formatting issues. You get a clear view of what’s missing and how to fix it—no guesswork.

3

Get Ranked & Hired

Receive your match score, actionable feedback, and optimized copy. Recruiters can rank multiple CVs at once and shortlist the best fits in minutes.
